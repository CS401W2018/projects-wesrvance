Interesting Facts

  • The family has a reality TV show.
  • Each sibling is a successful entrepreneur.
  • Known for their influence in fashion and beauty.
  • Frequent collaborators with major brands.
  • Active on social media with millions of followers.

Kim Kardashian is one of the most recognized members of the family. Known for her influence in fashion and beauty, she has launched multiple successful brands, including her own skincare and clothing lines.

Kourtney Kardashian, the eldest sibling, is known for her focus on wellness and lifestyle. She launched her own lifestyle brand, Poosh, which shares health and wellness tips with her followers.

Khloé Kardashian is known for her dedication to fitness and health. She has her own clothing line, Good American, which promotes body positivity and inclusivity in fashion.

Kendall Jenner, the supermodel of the family, has walked for some of the top fashion houses in the world. She is known for her presence in the modeling industry and has a successful career on the runway.

Kylie Jenner, the youngest sibling, is a self-made billionaire with her own beauty line, Kylie Cosmetics. Her influence in beauty has made her a major player in the industry.
