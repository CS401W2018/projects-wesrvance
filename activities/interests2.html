My Interest - Fijian Music

Interests Hero

Yanu Boyz

Yanu Boyz are a Fijian music band. They are one of the most popular ones. One of my favorite songs from them are Noqu Dina, Daru Dei, Moce Noqu Ka Talei, and Sa I Iko Ga.

InsideOut

InsideOut is another very popular band in Fijian Music. They are a more modernized and upbeat music. My favorite songs from them are Io, Ciri Yawa, and Naita.

Na Drua

This is another super popular band and actually my uncles. They sing some of their songs in English not just Fijian like the other bands I listed do. MY favorite songs from them are Be your man, Noqu Lewa, Ofa Anga, and Precious Love.
